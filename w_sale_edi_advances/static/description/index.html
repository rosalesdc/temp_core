Advanced advances for Mexican localization.

This module adds the functionality to relate advances that were previously created from a sales order. There are two approaches to deduct the advances, the first is to relate the advances generated in the final invoice, the second is not to relate them to the final invoice but to create an amendment for the total amount of the advances.

Features.

FYI: The following examples you will see, were created with Mexican localization in order to generated EDI documents and sign them into government fiscal entity.

There are two ways to deduct advances, deducting directly to an invoice or deducting generating a rectificative.

  • Deducting advances to a final invoice.
  • Generate a final invoice without deducting advances.
  • Relate advances from invoices.
Deducting advances to a final invoice.

In order to deduct advances that were created from a sales order into a final invoice you have to follow the next steps.

1.- Create advances in the sales order:

We must create advances from the sales order before creating the invoice for the quantity sold or delivered.

2.- Sign the advance.

The advance is of a single invoice line with the product configured to express the advances, it is stamped so that it can be deducted in the final invoice.

3.- Create final invoice and deducting advances.

When we invoiced the sales order, the advances that can be deducted in the final invoice will automatically be listed if the option to deduct advances is checked, at least one advance from the list must be marked, if the total of the selected advances exceeds the amount to invoice, will send a raise message indicating that it is not possible to deduct more than what will be invoiced. FYI: To obtain the advances they have to meet the following conditions:
  • Marked as an advance
  • It has to be posted
  • The SAT status has to be valid.
  • Having a fiscal folio.

4.- Final invoice with deducting advances..

In order to deduct the advances to the final invoice we take the sum of the untaxed amount of all advances and we obtain the advance percentage to be applied to each invoice line by the next operation: (amount advance / amount untaxed) * 100. This percentage is applied to the price subtotal of each line to get the price unit of the invoice line, if the line has quantity higher than 1 then we divide the (price unit / quantity) and the result is the price unit for the invoice line.

When the final invoice is created by deducting advances, the fiscal folio of the advances are taken and placed into the cfdi origin field with code 07 like this exampla: 07|4C585E0D-1AB3-4FAD-860C-5EA59DA16628,7ED7C7B6-AF87-48A4-8423-58BCD806BED6. is entered in the related invoices tab, the advances are listed.

Finally, the signed cfdi contains the fiscal folios of the advances related to the invoice.

Generate a final invoice without deducting advances.

If we have advances created in the sales order and they have been posted, signed can they meet all the conditions to be included at the final invoice. In this case, the field deduct advances is unchecked, if the advances that were previously created won't be deducted in the final invoice, then, they will be deducted in a refund invoice, a legend is displayed in the wizard indicating that there are advances that will be deducted but in a refund invoice, when the final invoice is posted.

The final invoice is created by the total amounts without deducting any advance at the odoo level, however the invoice will have the advances related from the cfdi origin field where the fiscal folios of the advances were placed, in the advances tab they are shown the related advances, as it is an invoice that did not deduct advances in the accounting, at the time the invoice was posted, the rectification of it is created automatically.

After the invoice is posted, we must relate the fiscal folio to the rectificative, we do it from the button that is displayed on one side of the fiscal folio field. In the related advances tab, the advances and the rectificative are listed.

After relating the fiscal folio to the rectificative, we must post it so that it can be signed, the sign cfdi will contain the fiscal folio related to the invoice from which this rectificative was created.

Once we have posted the rectificative, automatically it is added to the final invoice as a payment.

Relate advances from invoices.

This is another option to relate advances previously generated to an invoice, it is known that it doesn't have an accounting impact, it is only to realted the fiscal folios of the advances to an invoice.

To consider an invoice as an advance, you have to create an customer invoice and in draft state mark the field "is advance" then, post it, it has to be signed by the SAT.

Once you've generated the advances and you want to relate them to an invoice, you have to create a customer invoice and mark the field "Relate advances", if this field is marked a new page named "Related advances" is shown, on the lines you have to select the advances available which haven't been related to other invoices. You have to keep in mind that the advance invoices have to be valid by SAT and to have a fiscal folio otherwise they won't be available to be selected.

Once you have selected the advances on the lines, you have to relate the fiscal folios of the advances into the field "CFDI origin" using the button "Related folios", when you press it, the fiscal folios are taken and placed on the field and then the invoice will be able to be signed.

Once the invoice is signed the CFDI xml generated will have the fiscal folios related to the invoice.
