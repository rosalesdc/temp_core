Global Invoice MX EDI

Helps you to generate a global invoice.

Generate a global invoice.

In order to generate a global invoice we have to take into account the following conditions, fist; mark the check "Global invoice" at the invoice form and fill the rest of fields that are required when this field is marked.

  • Periodicity: Field required to record the period to which it corresponds the global voucher information. When the value of this field is "05" the Fiscal Regime field it should be "621".
  • Months: The key of the month or months to which corresponds to the information of the operations carried out with the public in general.
  • Year: The year to which the information corresponds to a Global Invoice. The registered value must be equal to the current year or the year immediately preceding considering the registered in the Date of issue of the voucher.

If you mark the invoice as a Global invoice and you select a partner tha does not have informatation that a global invoice requires, a message apprears at the invoice form, indicating that the Global invoice requires certain information to be generated.

If you have not corrected the information of the customer and try to confirm the invoice, valition message will be shown to avoid the post of the invoice and again, the message indicates that you have to correct the partner information.

If everything is correct, you confirm the invoice and if the informatation required for the CFDI is too correct, the CFDI with the global information node will be generated and sign by SAT.

At the CFDI(XML) the InformacionGlobal is added.
