Advanced invoice EDI cancellation

This module adds new features in the EDI cancellation process, for customer invoices.

Features.

FYI: The examples below were created using the Mexican localization to generate EDI documents and sign them at the government's fiscal entity

  • Reversal entry.
  • Substitution invoices.
  • Updated qty invoiced in sales order.
Reversal entry.

This module adds this new feature, every time the SAT cancels a customer invoice, a reversal entry is generated.

NOTE: Reversal entries are those made to reverse a previous entry. In a reversal entry, however, the accounts and the amounts assigned to them must be used, only the accounts that were previously placed as debits must now be placed as credits, and vice versa.

You post a customer invoice, it is signed by the SAT, for any reason you decide to cancel it, as the invoice has been signed by the SAT, you have to request the cancellation of the document, using the Request EDI Cancellation button.

Once the invoice is cancelled by SAT, a reversal entry is automatically created, in order to keep the accounting balanced in the period. The invoice is marked as reversed because the reversing entry is reconciled with the invoice.

Updated qty invoiced in sales order.

When the invoice is created from a sales order, it is signed by SAT and then, it is cancelled by SAT, we know that a reversal entry is created, it means that the invoice is cancelled and the qty invoiced on sale order lines has to discount the invoice lines quantities.

We have this sales order, for ordered qty by 1, we will create the invoice for quantity 1.

We can observe that the invoice quantity is for 1 and the invoice is related to the sales order.

On the sales order line, the qty invoiced is 1, due to the sales order has been invoiced, it is correct, now we will cancel the invoice.

The invoice is cancelled by SAT and a reversal entry is created and related to the invoice, it means the invoice is cancelled.

As the invoice has been cancelled by a reversal entry, the qty invoice on the sales order line is 0 and we can create another invoice for the sales order.

Substitution invoices.

This feature helps us to create a new invoice which substited the current invoice, if we got errors in the current invoice, we can to create the invoice that substitutes it. To create it, follow the next steps:

The button CREATE SUBSTITUTED INVOICE, launches an assistant that helps us to create the invoice. It will be shown, based on the following conditions:

  • The invoice payment state, has to be in: progress or paid.
  • The invoice does not be a substitution invoice.
  • The SAT status has to be, valid.
  • The invoice type has to be in: customer invoice or invoice credit note.
  • The invoice state has to be, posted.

If any of the conditions are not met, the button won't be shown.

Once the assistant is shown, a message appears on the body, that indicates us, an draft invoice will be created and, the fiscal folio of the current invoice, will be related to the substitution invoice using the code 04|fiscal folio, in the field CFDI Origin. If the current invoice which will be substitued by the new one, has to be the fiscal folio and signed by SAT, otherwise, we will get a raise error at the moment we try to create the substitution invoice, fiscal folio and valid SAT status are required in this case.

As you can see, the substitution invoice has the fiscal folio of the source invoice, due to we have to inform to SAT that this invoice substitutes the source invoice and it will be cancelled. A message is shown, indicating that this invoice is being substitued that invoice, once the current invoice is signed, it will be automatically related to the source invoice in the field Substituted By.

As we commented it, the substitution invoice was related to the source invoice in the field Substituted By, a message is shown, indicating that the current invoice has been substituted by that invoice, and it is required to cancel this one, in the cancellation process we used the reason 01, CFDI emitted with errors with relationship.

Now we have to request the cancellation for the invoice, using the button, Request Cancellation. If the invoice is correctly cancelled by SAT, the reversal entry is created and related to it, the invoice is marked as reversed due to the reversal entry. PAC and SAT status are stablished in cancelled.
